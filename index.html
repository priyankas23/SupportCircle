Self care activities? Empowerment Activities? You decide. Then check below for your results.

It's important to take a day off from your negative thoughts and do what you want. Don't worry about the consequences. Do things that are relaxing to you!"
"Some common self care activities include:
- Going on a walk
- Watch your favorite shows and movies
- Unplugging and spending time with yourself, your family, or a good book :)
- Exercising
- Listening to your favorite songs
Generally, do something that you enjoy and that isn't detrimental to your health. It'll make you feel so much better if you take some time to take care of yourself, and it won't be long before you're ready to face the world at full power again.
