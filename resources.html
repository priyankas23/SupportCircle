Our goal is to de-stigmatize mental health, especially among minorities.
We want to address the marginalized communities of society and promote mental health awareness. We want to help, support and empower these marginalized people when they might feel overlooked.
Mental health resources and special sites for women, lgbtq, and bipoc are very important to us.
